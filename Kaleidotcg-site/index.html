Kaleido TCG – Jersey City's Local Trading Card Hub

Kaleido TCG, located in Jersey City, specializes in Pokémon TCG, One Piece Card Game, Gundam Card Game, and Riftbound. We offer single-card sales, trade-ins, and PSA grading submission services, providing a reliable and welcoming space for all types of collectors.

It serves as a growing community hub for collectors, casual fans, and competitive players — a place where everyone can connect, play, and share their passion for trading cards.

Products & Services

Beyond trading cards, we also buy and sell sealed products, as well as a wide selection of card-related accessories such as binders, sleeves, deck boxes, and dices.

Whether you're building a deck, expanding your collection, or just starting out, we welcome you to stop by and explore our selection.

Trade-In Policy

We offer a simple and transparent trade-in structure:

  • 70% of market value for cash
  • 80% of market value for store credit

Bring your cards in anytime for an evaluation — we provide fair, professional, and efficient service.
